Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Deli turkey meat
  • Lettuce
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Honey mustard

Steps

  1. Lay the bread flat on a plate and spread mayo and honey mustard on both slices
  2. Place a generous amount of turkey meat on one slice of bread
  3. Add lettuce and tomato to the sandwich
  4. Add cheese to the sandwich
  5. Close the sandwich and cut in half
