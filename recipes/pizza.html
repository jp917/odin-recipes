Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Oregano
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Spread the pizza dough into a circle
  2. Add pizza sauce to pizza dough
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the pizza sauce
  4. Add oregano and garlic to taste
  5. Cook in oven on 400 degrees for 17 minutes
